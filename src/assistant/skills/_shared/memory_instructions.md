# Memory Module Instructions

## Cross-Domain Awareness

When making recommendations or providing insights, consider data from other domains:

- **Sleep recommendations** should account for fitness intensity and school load
- **Fitness planning** should consider calendar availability and sleep quality
- **Study scheduling** should account for workout recovery and energy levels
- **Social planning** should be aware of deadline pressure and wellness goals

## User Profile

Maintain awareness of user preferences and patterns learned over time:
- Preferred workout times and types
- Eating windows and meal preferences
- Study patterns and productivity times
- Social contact frequency preferences
- Financial budgeting habits

## Pattern Recognition

Identify and leverage recurring patterns:
- Weekly routines and schedules
- Seasonal variations in habits
- Correlations between different life areas (e.g., exercise and sleep quality)
- Progress trends toward goals
